About Project

Angels from Hell is a project dedicated to delving into the depths of horror and gothic, offering a unique visual and textual experience. With a dark and engaging atmosphere, our site brings the best of classic and contemporary horror, highlighting macabre stories, scary film reviews and a community passionate about the genre.
